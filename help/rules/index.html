obsidian mc rules

No Unauthorized Modifications: Maintain fairness and equality on the server by refraining from using mods or modifications that grant unfair advantages. Use only the Vive Craft mod and other approved enhancements.

No Offensive Language or Behavior: Keep conversations friendly and inclusive. Refrain from using offensive, discriminatory, or inappropriate language. Treat all players with respect and kindness.

No Disruptive Construction: While freedom is encouraged, please be mindful of your surroundings. Avoid constructing large and intrusive structures that may negatively impact the experience of others.

No Spamming or Advertising: Excessive chat messages, flooding, or advertising other servers or websites is not permitted. Engage in meaningful conversations and share relevant information.

No Unauthorized Account Use: Logging into other players' accounts without permission is strictly prohibited. Each player is responsible for their own account security.

teNo Physical Harm in VR: Be mindful of your physical surroundings while playing in VR. (keep it playable for vr players)
